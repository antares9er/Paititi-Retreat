Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Data Controller: G. Serina Neuner · PT. Montessori ERDKINDER Bali
Contact: admin@erdkinder-bali.com
Last updated: May 2026

1. Who We Are

Paititi Retreats is a sacred retreat event organised by G. Serina Neuner under PT. Montessori ERDKINDER Bali, located in Ubud, Bali, Indonesia. This privacy policy explains how we handle personal data collected through our website and booking process.

2. What Data We Collect

When you submit a booking request through our website, we collect the following information:

  • First and last name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Country of residence
  • Selected room / accommodation preference
  • Dietary requirements
  • Health considerations relevant to retreat participation
  • Any additional information you choose to share in the message field

We do not collect payment card details. Payments are processed externally via PayPal, which has its own privacy policy.

3. Why We Collect It

We collect this information solely to:

  • Process and confirm your retreat booking
  • Contact you with retreat details, schedules, and preparation materials
  • Ensure your safety and wellbeing during the retreat (dietary and health information)
  • Send payment confirmation and administrative communications

The legal basis for processing your data is the performance of a contract — you are providing this information in order to book a place at the retreat.

4. How Your Data Is Stored

Booking form submissions are processed through Formspree (formspree.io), a third-party form handling service. Submitted data is stored securely in the Formspree dashboard and delivered to our administrative email. Formspree is GDPR-compliant and their privacy policy can be found at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

Payment transactions via PayPal are handled entirely by PayPal and subject to PayPal's own privacy policy. We do not store or have access to your payment card details.

We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics tools on this website.

5. How Long We Keep Your Data

We retain your booking information for up to 12 months after the retreat date (19 July 2026) for administrative and accounting purposes, after which it is deleted. If you request earlier deletion, we will comply within 30 days.

6. Who We Share It With

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Your data may be shared only with:

  • Retreat facilitators and guides — solely to prepare for your arrival (name and dietary/health notes)
  • Formspree — as our form processing service
  • PayPal — when you choose to pay via PayPal

7. Your Rights

Under GDPR, if you are based in the European Union or European Economic Area, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion — request that we delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Objection — object to how we are using your data
  • Portability — receive your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, please email us at admin@erdkinder-bali.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information. Our website uses HTTPS encryption. Form submissions are handled by Formspree's secure infrastructure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at:

admin@erdkinder-bali.com
