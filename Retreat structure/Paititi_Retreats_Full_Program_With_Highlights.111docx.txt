Paititi Retreat — Full Retreat Program
8 Days / 7 Nights • Ubud, Bali • 12 – 19 July

This unique retreat is designed as a sacred inner journey — a gradual unfolding of the heart, the soul, and the deeper truth within your being.

Each day gently guides you deeper into healing, remembrance, balance, and transformation through ancient ceremonies, meditation, movement, sacred rituals, sound healing, crystal work, cacao rituals, and profound spiritual experiences inspired by both Balinese and Andean wisdom traditions.

The program flows in harmony with the natural rhythm of body, mind, and soul — creating space not only for transformation, but also for integration, rest, joy, beauty, and connection.

Healing Massages: As part of the retreat experience, each participant receives one individual 1-hour healing massage session with Ibu Jero Baba, included in the retreat package. Additional massage sessions may be available depending on the daily schedule and open spots.

Day 1 — Arrival & Opening the Sacred Space
Your journey begins in the peaceful jungle sanctuary of our Eco Art Village “Naya Veda Vastu” near Ubud.

After arrival and settling into the beautiful SKY Villa surrounded by lush tropical nature, the group gathers for the official opening circle of the retreat.

Together we enter the sacred field of the week through meditation, introductions, energetic alignment, and an opening transmission for the journey ahead.

The evening continues with a nourishing dinner and the possibility of receiving the first deeply healing Balinese massage by Ibu Jero Baba.
Highlights
* Welcome Circle & Introduction
* Opening Meditation
* Energetic Alignment
* Jungle Eco Village Experience
* Healing Massage Sessions

Day 2 — Water Purification & The Spirit of Cacao
Before sunrise, we journey to the sacred water temple area of Tirta Empul / Tampaksiring for a powerful Balinese purification (Melukat) and blessing ceremony guided by Ibu Jero Baba.

Water, in Balinese spirituality, represents cleansing, renewal, and spiritual rebirth.

After returning to the Eco Village for brunch and rest, the journey continues into the sacred world of cacao.

The group visits an organic cacao plantation and chocolate factory in Tabanan, where participants receive beautiful insights into the journey from organically grown cacao beans to handcrafted chocolate.

Later, back at the Eco Art Village, the connection with cacao deepens even further throughout the retreat.
Highlights
* Sacred Water Purification Ceremony
* Balinese Blessings
* Visit to Organic Cacao Plantation
* Chocolate Factory Tour
* Learning about the Sacred Cacao Process
* Healing Massage Sessions

Day 3 — Sacred Cacao & Heart Opening
This day is devoted to the spirit of cacao and the awakening of the heart.
After morning yoga, meditation and sound healing, participants enter a meditative ceremonial process with Daniel, learning how cacao beans are traditionally roasted over the fire, peeled by hand, and transformed into sacred ceremonial cacao paste.

The preparation itself becomes a ritual of mindfulness, gratitude, and presence.
In the afternoon, Daniel guides the group through a deeply heart-opening original Inka cacao ceremony — a sacred journey of connection, inner awakening, emotional healing, and expanded consciousness.
Held within a powerful ceremonial space, the spirit of cacao gently opens the heart, quiets the mind, and invites participants into deeper presence, reflection, soul connection, and visionary insight. Guided by ancient wisdom traditions, this experience becomes a profound invitation to reconnect with the wisdom of the heart and the deeper truth within.

Highlights
* Morning Yoga
* Meditation & Sound Healing
* Traditional Cacao Roasting Ritual
* Sacred Preparation of Ceremonial Cacao
* Inka Cacao Ceremony with Daniel
* Heart Opening & Reflection
* Healing Massage Sessions

Day 4 — Earth Wisdom & Sacred Offerings
This day honors the sacred connection between humanity, nature, spirit, and Mother Earth.
Participants learn how to create their own traditional Balinese temple offerings as well as Andean / Peruvian Pacha Mama offerings for sacred ceremonies and rituals.

In the afternoon, Daniel guides a profound shamanic Earth Ritual rooted in ancient Andean traditions — reconnecting participants with grounding, stability, ancestral wisdom, and the spirit of the Earth.
Highlights
* Create your own Balinese Offerings
* Create Andean / Pacha Mama Offerings
* Earth Ritual with Daniel
* Healing Massage Sessions

Day 5 — Sacred Rebirth & Inner Union
A deeply transformative day unfolds at a spectacular  sacred water temple 
Participants experience a deeply transformative Balinese spiritual rebirth ceremony at the sacred healing water temple of Tirta Taman Pechampuhan — a hidden sanctuary nestled within a lush jungle canyon surrounded by flowing waterfalls, ancient stone formations, and powerful natural spring waters.
Guided by Ibu Jero Baba, this sacred purification ritual invites participants into a profound process of cleansing, healing, renewal, and energetic rebalancing of the inner masculine and feminine energies. Held within the living spirit of nature and the sacred waters of Bali, the ceremony becomes an intimate journey of release, remembrance, and reconnection with one’s authentic soul essence.

Later in Ubud, participants take part in a beautiful Silver workshop where each person creates their own sacred “Self-Love Ring” for the intimate ceremony at the ocean the following day.
Highlights
* Sacred Rebirth Ceremony
* Silver Workshop
* Create your own Self-Love Ring
* Group Reflection & Meditation

Day 6 — The Self-Love Marriage Ceremony
One of the most intimate and unforgettable moments of the retreat.
Before sunrise, the group journeys to a breathtaking beach in East Bali.
Together with all three guides, participants co-create a sacred altar by the ocean before entering the deeply moving Self-Love Marriage Ceremony — a ceremonial union of the inner feminine and masculine energies within oneself.
This is not a traditional marriage ceremony between two people.
It is a sacred commitment to yourself, your soul, your heart, your healing, and your authentic path in life.
The ceremony is lovingly guided by G. Serina Neuner, Daniel, and Ibu Jero Baba — each weaving their own spiritual gifts, healing presence, wisdom traditions, and ceremonial talents into this unique shared experience.
After the ceremony, participants celebrate life at the ocean — swimming, relaxing, connecting, reflecting, and simply enjoying the beauty of Bali.
Hightlights
* Beach Ceremony in East Bali
* Sacred Ocean Altar Creation
* Self-Love Marriage Ceremony
* Celebration of Life & Self

Day 7 — Crystal Healing & Shamanic Fire Ceremony
This day focuses on transformation, healing and energetic activation of the Cosmic Human Being.
Daniel guides participants through powerful Crystal Healing work and Shamanic practices designed to support emotional release, energetic cleansing, inner clarity, and spiritual awakening.
In the evening, the retreat culminates in a Sacred Shamanic Fire Ritual — a ceremonial space to release old patterns, fears, limitations, and emotional burdens into the transforming power of fire.
A night of deep transformation, remembrance, and renewal.
Highlights
* Crystal Healing Work with Daniel
* Shamanic Healing Practices
* Sacred Fire Ceremony
* Emotional Release & Renewal

Day 8 — Integration & Farewell
The final morning is dedicated to reflection, gratitude, integration, meditation, and sharing.
Participants are gently guided through the closing process of the retreat — integrating the experiences, insights, healing, and transformations of the past days.
The retreat concludes with a heartfelt farewell lunch  surrounded by the peaceful energy of the jungle sanctuary.
Many participants leave not only rested — but deeply transformed, reconnected with themselves, and inspired to walk life with greater clarity, authenticity, love, and inner alignment.
Highlights
* Final Meditation & Sound Healing
* Group Reflection
* Integration & Closing Circle
* Farewell Lunch
