Questions

Frequently Asked

Who are Paititi Retreats for?

Our retreats are crafted for women who are ready for a deep and sacred transformation — whether you are new to the spiritual path or a seasoned practitioner. All you need is an open heart and a genuine readiness to meet yourself fully.

Where are the retreats held?

Our retreats take place in carefully selected luxury sanctuary spaces — from lush jungle settings to serene mountain escapes. All locations are shared upon application to ensure the right fit for each group.

What does the investment include?

Your investment includes all luxury accommodation, all plant-based meals and ceremonial teas, all guided ceremonies and healing sessions, your pre-retreat preparation materials, and 30 days of post-retreat integration support. International flights are not included.

How do I know if this retreat is right for me?

We offer a complimentary sacred discovery call for all prospective guests. This is a beautiful space for us to connect, understand your intentions, answer questions, and discern together whether this retreat is your next step. There is no pressure — only genuine care for your highest good.

Are payment plans available?

Yes. We believe a genuine calling should never be limited by financial constraint alone. We offer flexible, interest-free payment plans for all retreat programmes. Please mention this during your discovery call and we will create something that works beautifully for you.

What is your cancellation policy?

Deposits are non-refundable and secure your place. Full payments made more than 60 days in advance are 75% refundable. Within 60 days we offer a credit to a future retreat. In cases of genuine emergency, each situation is handled with compassion and care.
