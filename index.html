Our Story

A Temple of
Sacred Transformation

Paititi Retreats was born from a sacred calling — to build a bridge between ancient wisdom traditions and the deeper truth within each soul. Inspired by Paititi, the legendary Golden City of Light from the ancestral Inka traditions of the Andes and the Amazon, we craft retreat experiences as rare and precious as the legend itself.

For the very first time, ancestral Inka wisdom and ancient Balinese spiritual traditions come together in one unique retreat experience — a sacred fusion of worlds, cultures, and consciousness held within the lush jungle sanctuary of Ubud, Bali.

Through ceremony, shadow work, meditation, yoga, sacred cacao, crystal healing, and deep inner processes, each participant is guided step by step toward remembrance, healing, and authentic transformation.

The Full Retreat Program

A day-by-day journey — from arrival to farewell, each day carefully designed to guide you deeper into healing, remembrance, and authentic transformation.

Each participant receives one individual 1-hour healing massage with Ibu Jero Baba, included in the retreat package. Additional sessions may be available.

01 Day

Arrival & Opening the Sacred Space

Your journey begins in the peaceful jungle sanctuary of Naya Veda Vastu eco-village near Ubud. After settling into the beautiful SKY Villa surrounded by lush tropical nature, the group gathers for the official opening circle — entering the sacred field through meditation, introductions, energetic alignment, and an opening transmission for the journey ahead. The evening continues with a nourishing dinner and the first healing Balinese massage by Ibu Jero Baba.

  • Welcome Circle & Introduction
  • Opening Meditation
  • Energetic Alignment
  • Jungle Eco Village Experience
  • Healing Massage Sessions
02 Day

Water Purification & The Spirit of Cacao

Before sunrise, we journey to the sacred water temple of Tirta Empul / Tampaksiring for a powerful Balinese purification (Melukat) and blessing ceremony guided by Ibu Jero Baba — cleansing, renewal, and spiritual rebirth through sacred water. After returning for brunch and rest, the afternoon takes the group to an organic cacao plantation and chocolate factory in Tabanan, revealing the journey from bean to sacred ceremonial chocolate.

  • Sacred Water Purification Ceremony
  • Balinese Blessings
  • Visit to Organic Cacao Plantation
  • Chocolate Factory Tour
  • Learning about the Sacred Cacao Process
  • Healing Massage Sessions
03 Day

Sacred Cacao & Heart Opening

Devoted to the spirit of cacao and the awakening of the heart. After morning yoga, meditation, and sound healing, participants enter a meditative ceremonial process with Daniel — learning how cacao beans are traditionally roasted over fire, peeled by hand, and transformed into sacred ceremonial cacao paste. In the afternoon, Daniel guides the group through an original Inka Cacao Ceremony: a profound journey of inner awakening, emotional healing, and expanded consciousness.

  • Morning Yoga
  • Meditation & Sound Healing
  • Traditional Cacao Roasting Ritual
  • Sacred Preparation of Ceremonial Cacao
  • Inka Cacao Ceremony with Daniel
  • Heart Opening & Reflection
  • Healing Massage Sessions
04 Day

Earth Wisdom & Sacred Offerings

This day honours the sacred connection between humanity, nature, spirit, and Mother Earth. Participants learn to create their own traditional Balinese temple offerings and Andean / Peruvian Pacha Mama offerings for sacred ceremonies. In the afternoon, Daniel guides a profound shamanic Earth Ritual rooted in ancient Andean traditions — reconnecting participants with grounding, stability, ancestral wisdom, and the spirit of the Earth.

  • Create your own Balinese Offerings
  • Create Andean / Pacha Mama Offerings
  • Earth Ritual with Daniel
  • Healing Massage Sessions
05 Day

Sacred Rebirth & Inner Union

A deeply transformative day at the hidden water temple of Tirta Taman Pechampuhan — a jungle canyon sanctuary of flowing waterfalls and ancient stone formations. Guided by Ibu Jero Baba, a sacred rebirth ceremony invites participants into a profound process of cleansing, renewal, and energetic rebalancing of the inner masculine and feminine. Later in Ubud, each participant creates their own sacred Self-Love Ring in a traditional silver workshop — in preparation for the ceremony at the ocean the following day.

  • Sacred Rebirth Ceremony
  • Silver Workshop in Ubud
  • Create your own Self-Love Ring
  • Group Reflection & Meditation
06 Day

The Self-Love Marriage Ceremony

One of the most intimate and unforgettable moments of the retreat. Before sunrise, the group journeys to a breathtaking beach in East Bali. Together with all three guides, participants co-create a sacred altar by the ocean before entering the deeply moving Self-Love Marriage Ceremony — a sacred commitment to yourself, your soul, your heart, and your authentic path in life. Guided by G. Serina Neuner, Daniel, and Ibu Jero Baba. After the ceremony, the afternoon is given to celebration, swimming, and joy at the ocean.

  • Sunrise Beach Ceremony · East Bali
  • Sacred Ocean Altar Creation
  • Self-Love Marriage Ceremony
  • Celebration of Life & Self
07 Day

Crystal Healing & Shamanic Fire Ceremony

A day of transformation, healing, and energetic activation of the Cosmic Human Being. Daniel guides participants through powerful Crystal Healing work and Shamanic practices designed to support emotional release, energetic cleansing, and inner clarity. In the evening, the retreat culminates in a Sacred Shamanic Fire Ritual — a ceremonial space to release old patterns, fears, limitations, and emotional burdens into the transforming power of fire.

  • Crystal Healing Work with Daniel
  • Shamanic Healing Practices
  • Sacred Fire Ceremony
  • Emotional Release & Renewal
08 Day

Integration & Farewell

The final morning is dedicated to reflection, gratitude, integration, meditation, and sharing. Participants are gently guided through the closing process of the retreat — integrating the experiences, insights, healing, and transformations of the past eight days. The retreat concludes with a heartfelt farewell lunch surrounded by the peaceful energy of the jungle sanctuary. Many participants leave not only rested — but deeply transformed, reconnected with themselves, and inspired to walk life with greater clarity, authenticity, love, and inner alignment.

  • Final Meditation & Sound Healing
  • Group Reflection
  • Integration & Closing Circle
  • Farewell Lunch

Questions

Frequently Asked

Who is this retreat for?

This retreat is for souls who feel called toward authentic transformation — whether you are at the beginning of your spiritual path or have walked this journey for many years. No previous spiritual experience is needed. Only an open heart and the genuine willingness to meet yourself fully.

Where is the retreat held?

The retreat takes place at a Jungle Eco Village in Pejeng, just outside Ubud, Bali — 15 to 20 minutes from central Ubud. Participants stay in the breathtaking SKY Villa, a bamboo architectural masterpiece with a 12-metre infinity pool and panoramic jungle views. The eco-village spans two hectares and includes organic gardens, a yoga shala, a sound dome, and walking meditation paths.

What does the investment include?

Your investment includes all accommodation in the SKY Villa, all plant-based meals and ceremonial teas, all guided ceremonies and healing sessions, a visit to an organic cacao plantation and chocolate factory, a traditional Balinese silver workshop, one individual healing massage with Ibu Jero Baba, the sacred Otonan Ceremony, pre-retreat preparation materials, and 30 days of post-retreat integration support. International flights are not included.

How do I know if this retreat is right for me?

Simply send us an email and we will personally write back to you. We take the time to understand your intentions, answer your questions, and help you discern whether this retreat is your next step. There is no pressure — only genuine care for your highest good.

Are payment plans available?

A 30% non-refundable deposit secures your place, with the remaining balance due no later than 30 days before the retreat begins. We accept payment by Credit Card (Stripe), PayPal, or Bank Transfer. If you need a different arrangement due to financial circumstances, mention this in your email enquiry and we will do our best to support you.

What is your cancellation policy?

The 30% deposit is non-refundable in all cases. Beyond that: cancellation more than 30 days before the retreat — any payments beyond the deposit are refunded in full; cancellation between 14 and 30 days — 50% of the total price is refunded; cancellation less than 14 days — no refund can be issued. You may transfer your booking to another person with prior agreement. If the retreat does not reach the minimum of 8 participants, all payments made are fully refunded. We strongly recommend comprehensive travel insurance.
